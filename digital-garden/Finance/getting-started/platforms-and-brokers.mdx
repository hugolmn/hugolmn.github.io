## Degiro
**Available accounts:** CTO

The Pros:
- Wide choice of stocks and ETFs.
- Transaction fee: 2€ for French, Canadian and US stocks.
- Preview of impending dividends.
- Detailed charts and company data.

The Cons:
- Significant increase in transaction fees: from 0.5€ to 1€ in 2022, to 2€ in 2023.
- Foreign broker: account has to be declared when filing your taxes.
- No IFU (Imprimé Fiscal Unique) that would simplify filing your taxes.


**Conclusion**: I no longer recommend Degiro.
